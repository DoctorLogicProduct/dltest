Meet DoctorLogic

DoctorLogic is an all-in-one website marketing platform created exclusively for healthcare providers. The DoctorLogic software was invented to power custom-designed websites for patient acquisition, retention marketing, and practice insights that help providers connect with more people.

About DoctorLogic

DoctorLogic was formed officially in May of 2013 - but in many ways the history and culture of our company goes back much, much further. The founders of the company have worked together building successful companies since 1999.

DoctorLogic offers custom website designs, as well as market proven design options and have created a digital marketing platform that allows users to manage leads, website content, PPC, reputation, galleries, and SEO while providing website analytics from a single, web-based software program.
